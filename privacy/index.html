Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Region: India (Play Store)

Spends("the app") is a local-first financial decision-support tool for Android. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

Data we process

  • Bank & UPI notifications (primary, optional): When you enable the notification listener, the app reads alerts from approved financial apps. Only a short snippet (max 80 characters) and a content hash are stored.
  • Financial SMS (secondary, optional): Parsed on-device when you grant SMS permission after in-app disclosure.
  • SMS history import (optional, one-time): Last 90 days only, on-device.
  • Manual data: income baseline, goals, transactions you enter, and intervention feedback.

We do not upload or parse bank account statements (PDF/CSV).

Data we do not collect

  • We do not upload SMS or transaction data to servers.
  • We do not sell or share financial data with advertisers.
  • We do not read OTP or non-financial personal messages.

Storage & retention

On Android, data is stored in encrypted on-device storage (AndroidX EncryptedSharedPreferences). Cloud backup is disabled. Data persists until you use Settings → Delete all data or uninstall.

Your rights

  • Access — view data in Transactions and Settings.
  • Correction — edit categories and merchants inline.
  • Deletion — Settings → Delete all data (irreversible).

Children

Spends is not directed at children under 18.

Changes

We will update this policy before Play Store releases that change data practices.
